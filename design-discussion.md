## Based on Feedback from 2026-07
The biggest feedbacks I heard where 
- no group hiding (makes it too snow ball?) 
- game was too easy (but the space was much smaller too) 
- rounds way too long (people getting bored and preferred many short games) 
- people want to move between photos (I’m really iffy on this one) 
- points were way too easy to earn for initial seeker and hiders not rewarded enough for surviving (and point ear by 15 min intervals were too long)

## Design Discussion & Game Levers
### Intended
- Cut the game design in half: 30 min hide + 30 min x 4 rounds -> 15 hide + 45 seek
- Lever: Starting Seekers accelerate the game.
